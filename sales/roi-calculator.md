# ROI Framework — Calculating the Return on Investing in Simba

## Overview

Investing in a marketing mix modeling platform should itself deliver measurable returns. This framework helps you estimate the ROI of adopting Simba based on common value drivers.

## Value Drivers

### 1. Media Spend Optimization

The primary value of MMM is identifying where marketing spend is efficient and where it's wasted. Even modest improvements create significant returns.

**Calculation framework**:

| Metric | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Annual media spend | $10,000,000 |
| Estimated waste identified by MMM | 10–20% (industry benchmarks) |
| Recoverable spend or incremental revenue | $1,000,000 – $2,000,000 |

Most organizations find that **5–15% of their media spend** can be reallocated to higher-performing channels after MMM insights, producing measurable incremental revenue.

### 2. Replacing Expensive Consulting Studies

Traditional econometric studies from consulting firms typically cost $100K–$500K and take 3–6 months. They're also point-in-time — by the time results arrive, the market has moved.

| Metric | Consulting | Simba |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Cost per study | $100K–$500K | Subscription (fraction) |
| Frequency | 1–2x/year | Continuous |
| Turnaround | 3–6 months | Hours to days |
| Annual cost for continuous measurement | $200K–$1M+ | Simba subscription |

### 3. Faster Decision-Making

Speed has economic value. Being able to re-optimize budgets in hours rather than months means:

- Catching underperforming campaigns earlier
- Responding to market changes faster
- Reducing the "lag tax" of slow measurement cycles

### 4. Reduced Analytics Headcount Requirements

Simba's no-code interface and smart defaults reduce the need for dedicated data scientists to build and maintain MMM models.

| Scenario | Without Simba | With Simba |
|----------|--------------|------------|
| Building a model | Data scientist (weeks) | Marketing analyst (hours) |
| Updating a model | Data scientist (days) | Automated with new data |
| Running scenarios | Custom analysis (days) | Self-service (minutes) |

### 5. Better Cross-Channel Allocation

Platform-reported metrics (Meta ROAS, Google CPA) overcount conversions because they can't see the full picture. Simba provides de-duplicated, incremental measurement across all channels — leading to better allocation decisions.

## Estimating Your ROI

### Simple Formula

```
ROI of Simba = (Value of improved spend allocation + Consulting savings + Productivity gains) / Simba subscription cost
```

### Conservative Example

| Value Driver | Annual Value |
|-------------|-------------|
| 5% spend optimization on $5M budget | $250,000 |
| Replacing 1 consulting study | $150,000 |
| Analyst time savings (0.5 FTE) | $50,000 |
| **Total value** | **$450,000** |
| Simba subscription | Contact for pricing |

Even conservative estimates typically show **10–50x ROI** on the platform investment.

## How to Build Your Business Case

1. **Quantify your media spend** — What's your total annual media investment?
2. **Estimate optimization opportunity** — Assume 5–10% can be better allocated
3. **Count current measurement costs** — Consulting fees, analyst time, tool costs
4. **Calculate decision speed value** — How much does slow measurement cost you?
5. **Compare to Simba subscription** — Contact sales for pricing specific to your needs
