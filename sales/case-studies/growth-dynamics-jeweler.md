# Case Study: Growth Dynamics × High-End Jeweler

## Overview

Growth Dynamics, a marketing analytics agency, partnered with a high-end jeweler to transform their marketing measurement from periodic retrospective reports into a continuous, strategic guidance system powered by Simba.

## The Challenge

The jeweler was operating with a common marketing measurement setup:

- **Quarterly reporting cycles** — By the time insights arrived, the market had moved
- **Fragmented measurement** — Online and in-store sales were measured separately with different tools
- **Lack of transparency** — Previous modeling outputs were black-box numbers that the client couldn't interrogate
- **Limited actionability** — Reports showed what happened, but didn't guide what to do next

## The Solution

Growth Dynamics deployed Simba to build a unified marketing mix model covering the jeweler's full media mix and both online and brick-and-mortar sales channels.

### Key Implementation Details

- **Omnichannel modeling** — A single model capturing both online revenue and in-store sales
- **Weekly cadence** — Models updated weekly instead of quarterly, enabling real-time strategic guidance
- **Glass-box transparency** — The client could inspect model assumptions, building trust in the results
- **Scenario planning** — Budget scenarios presented to the client before spend decisions were made

## The Results

| Metric | Before Simba | With Simba |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| **Reporting cadence** | Quarterly | Weekly |
| **Channel coverage** | Online only | Omnichannel (online + brick-and-mortar) |
| **Transparency** | Black-box | Full glass-box — inspectable priors and parameters |
| **Client relationship** | Report recipient | Strategic partner |

### Key Outcomes

- **Weekly strategic guidance** — Moved from backward-looking quarterly reports to forward-looking weekly recommendations
- **Omnichannel visibility** — For the first time, the client could see the full impact of marketing across online and physical retail
- **Client trust** — Glass-box transparency eliminated the "trust me" dynamic and built genuine confidence in the numbers
- **Agency differentiation** — Growth Dynamics elevated their positioning from analytics vendor to strategic advisor

## Why Simba Made the Difference

1. **Speed** — Weekly model updates would have been impractical with traditional consulting approaches
2. **Transparency** — The jeweler's leadership needed to see and understand the methodology to trust the recommendations
3. **Omnichannel capability** — Modeling both online and brick-and-mortar in one framework was essential for a retailer with significant physical presence
4. **Scenario planning** — The ability to model budget scenarios together transformed client conversations from reporting to strategy
