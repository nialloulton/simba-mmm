# Data Sovereignty — Data Residency and International Transfers

For global brands, understanding where your data is stored and how it's transferred across borders is critical. Simba provides clear policies on data residency and international data transfers.

## Data Residency

Your marketing data is stored in secure AWS data centers. Data residency requirements are handled through:

- **Infrastructure location** — Data is hosted in AWS regions that align with your requirements
- **Isolated storage** — Per-customer S3 buckets ensure your data remains separate and identifiable
- **Enterprise options** — Enterprise tier customers can discuss dedicated hosting arrangements

## International Data Transfers

When data is transferred outside of your jurisdiction, Simba uses appropriate safeguards:

### Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)

- EU-approved Standard Contractual Clauses are used for international data transfers
- SCCs ensure that personal data receives adequate protection regardless of destination
- This is the standard mechanism used by major enterprises for cross-border data flows

### Transfer Safeguards

- All international transfers are encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
- Access controls ensure that data is only accessible by authorized personnel
- Processing agreements govern how data is handled at every stage

## For Enterprise Customers

Enterprise tier customers have additional options:

- **Dedicated hosting** — Discuss custom hosting arrangements to meet specific data residency requirements
- **Custom data processing agreements** — Tailored agreements for specific regulatory requirements
- **Compliance documentation** — Additional documentation for your internal compliance teams
